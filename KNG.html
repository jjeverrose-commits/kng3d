About
The story behind KNG 3D Printing

Family‑run micro workshop

KNG 3D Printing is a tiny, neon‑lit corner of the world where a kid and his grandad build things together. Kain handles the digital side: modelling, slicing, and running the printer. Grandad brings engineering experience, measurements, and safety checks.

From idea to object

Whether it’s a missing plastic clip, a custom name tag, or a small desk gadget, we focus on practical, everyday prints. We like parts that solve problems, make life easier, or feel special because they’re made just for you.
